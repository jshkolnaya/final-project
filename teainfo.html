Starting from the Basics

All true teas are made from the leaves of a plant called Camellia Sinensis. There are also herbal teas, which aren't considered true teas because they don't come from the Camellia Sinensis plant. True teas all have some amount of caffeine, with the amount depending on how the leaves are processed, while herbal teas typically don't have any caffeine.
